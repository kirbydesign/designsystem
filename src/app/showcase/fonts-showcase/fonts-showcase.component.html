Font sizes can be set by either using the font classes, e.g. .kirby-text-large, or by using the tags like h1, h2 & h3.

Font can also be set with the font-size function directly on your own classes, e.g:

H1 | .kirby-text-xlarge

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

H2 | .kirby-text-large

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

H3 | .kirby-text-medium

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

P | .kirby-text-normal

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

.kirby-text-small

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

.kirby-text-xsmall

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

.kirby-text-xxsmall

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

$font-sizes: (
  xl:  32px,
  l:  22px,
  m:  18px,
  n:  16px,
  s:  14px,
  xs: 12px,
  xxs: 10px
)
