Giving a card any click event (click)="function()" will change your cursor to a pointer when hovering the card. To ensure that the card is accessible by keyboard, the card gets a tab-index so it can be focused by tabbing. To mirror a button's behavior, any registered click-event will also be called by pressing enter and space when the card is focused.

If mode="flat" has been given to the card, it will automatically be reverted to default elevation.
