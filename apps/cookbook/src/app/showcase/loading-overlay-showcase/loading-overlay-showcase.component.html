The loading overlay is available both as a component and as a service.

Component

The loading overlay component will add the overlay on top of the content that is slotted into the component.

Properties

Service

The service will show a full page loading overlay. It provides two methods: showLoadingOverlay & hideLoadingOverlay . showLoadingOverlay takes two arguments:

  1. showBackdrop: boolean - Shows the transparent loading overlay.
  2. hideContent: boolean - Shows the loading overlay with 100% opacity.

hideLoadingOverlay is required to dismiss the overlay. It takes no arguments.
