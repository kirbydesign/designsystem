List with no background shape:

Setting shape="none" on a Kirby List removes the background, border and shadow from the list and the list item containers to provide flexibility

Simple list with no shape

Detailed card with list no shape

The no shape list should be used when the list is within a card that also has other content. This is removing the otherwise shown shadow around the list.
